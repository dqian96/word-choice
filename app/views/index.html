ABOUT

Do you find your essay to be boring, repetitive, or bland? Maybe you need to spice it up by improving your word choice? Diction is an important part of writing. Using a wide variety of words helps create more vivid imagery for the reader. At Word Choice, we allow you to do just that - diversify your writing.

Word Choice is a tool that analyzes submitted articles and suggests synonms for frequently used words. A histogram is created in order to help users visualize their data and a list of synonms is generated thanks to the thesaurus service provided by words.bighugelabs.com. Please be courteous with the number and size of article submissions as we have limited API calls!

Get started now by clicking "Analyze your writing" on the header or by creating an account. By creating an account, Word Choice can track your most frequently used words.

Word Choice is created by David Qian, a second year software engineering student at the University of Waterloo. This webapp is built on the MEAN stack and source code is avaliable under the MIT license. Third party tools such as Chart JS, Animate.css and Material Design concepts were used in this product.
